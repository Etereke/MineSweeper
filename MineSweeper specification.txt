Console minesweeper

	-3 difficulties + custom:
		-Beginner: 9x9, 10 mines
		-Intermediate: 16x16, 40 mines
		-Expert: 16x30, 99 mines
		-Custom: min 9x9, max 24x30; min 10 mines, max 90% mines

	-2 types of control
		-With marker: you have a marker on board which you can move with arrows, 
				choose to flag, reveal, or mass-reveal current field
		-With commands: you can enter commands flag, reveal and the coordinates of a field

	-Menu: new game, instructions, quit; new game -> choose difficulty
	-Additional commands during game: back to menu, toggle control mode
